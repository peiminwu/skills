# Source Priority

Use the source list below as the starting discovery set:

- [source-list](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/peiminwu/source-list/refs/heads/main/list)

Prefer the following source order:

1. Official regulators, exchanges, courts, and company filings
2. Named short-seller or activist research firms
3. Major financial media
4. Reputable archives or mirrors when the original page is gone

## Notes

- Search both Chinese and English names when the company has overseas listings or offshore entities.
- Prefer exact-date documents over retrospective summaries.
- When sources conflict, keep the stronger source and describe the conflict briefly.
